BLACKPINK

Overview

Blackpink is a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment, consisting of members Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. Since their debut in 2016, Blackpink has become one of the most popular and influential kpop groups globally.

Members

Jisoo

Kim Ji-soo, known as Jisoo, is a vocalist and visual of Blackpink.

Jennie

Kim Jennie, known as Jennie, is a lead rapper and vocalist of Blackpink.

Rosé

Roseanne Park, known as Rosé, is a main vocalist and lead dancer of Blackpink.

Lisa

Lalisa Manoban, known as Lisa, is a main rapper and lead dancer of Blackpink.

Industry Impact

Blackpink has significantly impacted the kpop industry, pioneering a new era of global recognition for kpop music. They have shattered numerous records, including being the first kpop girl group to perform at and headline Coachella and amassing billions of views on their music videos on YouTube. Blackpink has embarked on several world tours, including the "In Your Area" and "Born Pink" tours, which spanned across Asia, North America, Europe, and Oceania. Their music sales include numerous hit singles and albums, with millions of records sold worldwide.
